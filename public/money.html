How this works

  1. The three numbers up top: saved today, saved this quarter, and saved all-time — what we've saved you. They go up as you act.
  2. Click a decision in the list (the one with the red border). See the proof, then pick what to do — each option shows how we'll check it worked.
  3. We check it one of three ways: your data confirms it, you attach proof, or you confirm it yourself (for things we can't track) — always labelled clearly.
  4. Open Tracking to see who's handling what and where it stands; use Open in Dashboard to see all the raw numbers.
